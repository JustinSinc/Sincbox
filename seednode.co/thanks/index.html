Acknowledgements The tools keeping this site running

Apache

The world's most widely-used web server, serving an estimated 50% of all websites.
Serves our primary website landing page at seednode.co/.

Nginx

A free, open-source, high-performance HTTP server and reverse proxy.
Directs requests to the proper service, and provides SSL termination for all services.

Subsonic

A Java-based streaming media server, with an easy-to-use web interface.
Provides our music streaming service at seednode.co/music/.

Codiad

A web-based IDE framework with a small footprint and minimal requirements.
Provides our hosted IDE service at seednode.co/code/.

The Lounge

A self-hosted web-based IRC client forked from the shout-irc project.
Provides our web-IRC service at seednode.co/irc/.

Alpine Linux

A security-oriented, lightweight Linux distribution based on musl libc and busybox.
Used as the base distribution for most of our services, and for our hosted containers.

KVM

A full virtualization solution for Linux on x86 hardware supporting device passthrough.
The hypervisor platform for several of our hosted services, as well as our VPS service.

LXC

An operating-system virtualization method for running multiple isolated Linux systems on a shared kernel.
Used with Docker as the base system for most of our services, and for our hosted containers.

GoTTY

A simple command line tool that turns your CLI tools into web applications.
Provides Sinc's live terminal broadcasts at seednode.co/watchme/, and the
web-based containers at seednode.co/docker/.

HTML5UP

Free, responsive HTML5 templates licensed under the Creative Commons.
Our landing page uses Strongly Typed.

Quackit

A website providing free web design and development resources.
This acknowledgements page uses their Portfolio, 2 Column template.

Let's Encrypt

A free, automated, and open Certificate Authority, promoting universal TLS support.
All of the services we provide are encrypted through Let's Encrypt certificates.

Guacamole

A clientless remote desktop gateway, supporting protocols like VNC and RDP.
Provides the RDP gateways at seednode.co/guacamole/.

Lychee

A free photo management tool, with private album support and EXIF info display.
Provides the photo galleries at seednode.co/photos/.
