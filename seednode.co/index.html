The spiritual successor to seedhost.

I offer free Linux containers and occasionally virtual machines to those looking to further their knowledge of networking, systems administration, or scripting.
By default, all containers are running Alpine Linux; if you requirement a specific distribution, let me know and we can work something out.
All virtualized guests are NATed; you can choose your own port for SSH so long as it's not taken by another user.
Set your web server to listen on port 80, and seednode.co/username/ will direct to your page.
Web services can be assigned their own custom path, i.e. seednode.co/foo/, per request.
